Odin Recipes

Delicious swedish chokladbollar, easy and quick to make. A favorite amongs children

Ingrediens

  • Oats
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Shredded Coconut
  • A tiny bit of coffie
  • Powdered vanilla
    •

    Directions

    1. Mix everything in a bowl
    2. Make round balls and cover them in shredded Coconut
    3. Let them rest for 30 mins in the refrigerator
