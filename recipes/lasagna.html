A tasty and easy to do lasagna that only needs a few ingrediens

Ingrediens

  • Miced Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Union
  • Vegtable oil
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna plates
    •

    Directions

    1. Cook and stir the miced meat with miced onion
    2. Pre heat the oven to 200 degrees
    3. When the meat is cooked put some salt
    4. Put the tomato sauce over the meat
    5. Make some bachamel sauce
    6. Put the lasagna plates with meat on top and tomato sauce, finish with cheese
    7. Put it in the oven for 20 minutes
